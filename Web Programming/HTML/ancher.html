Cricket

Cricket is one of the most popular sports in India. It is played between two teams, and each team has eleven players. The game is played using a bat, a ball, and wickets. Cricket teaches us teamwork, discipline, and patience. It also improves physical fitness and mental concentration. Many people enjoy watching cricket matches, and famous players inspire young generations to play this game.



















Football

Football is a world-famous sport played in almost every country. It is played between two teams of eleven players each. The main aim of the game is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent’s goalpost. Football improves stamina, speed, and coordination. It also teaches teamwork and sportsmanship. The game is loved by people of all ages.



















Hockey

Hockey is the national sport of India. It is played using a hockey stick and a small hard ball. Two teams try to score goals by hitting the ball into the opponent’s goal. Hockey requires speed, skill, and quick decision-making. India has a proud history in hockey and has won many international tournaments. The game promotes teamwork and physical fitness.
